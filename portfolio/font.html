Donald J Trump

Donald John Trump (born June 14, 1946) is an American media personality, businessman, and politician who served as the 45th president of the United States from January 20, 2017, to January 20, 2021. Before entering politics, he was a real estate developer and television personality. He is the chairman and president of The Trump Organization, which is the principal holding company for his real estate and other business ventures. Trump has been involved in various business ventures, including hotels, casinos, golf courses, and branding. He also hosted the reality TV show "The Apprentice" for several years. Trump's presidency was marked by significant policy changes and controversies, both domestically and internationally.
